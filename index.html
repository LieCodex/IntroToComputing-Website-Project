About Me

I am a self taught game developer, I started learning game dev when i was on grade 10 using the unity Game engine, since it is the most famous therefor it also has the most amount of tutorial online, I haven't made any significance progress during that time because my school and game learning time is split apart. My passion since then is making games, i have made a couple of unfinished games as a training but dropped game dev for a while due to burn out.

I came back to game dev on grade 12, since we were tasked to make an educational game for our research, Thankfully due to my background in game dev and with the help of a ton and i mean ton of youtube videos, I manage to make one, it is titled Spell Bound.
